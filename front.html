Simon Says

Game Component:

  • A round device with four colored buttons (usually: red, blue, green, and yellow).
  • Each button lights up and plays a unique tone.

How to play

  1. Simon lights up one button.
  2. The player presses the same button.

  4. The player must repeat the entire sequence in order.

  6. If the player presses the wrong button, the game ends (or starts over, depending on the mode).
